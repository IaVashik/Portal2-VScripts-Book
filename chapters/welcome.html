Welcome!

This book is your comprehensive guide to creating custom gameplay in Portal 2 using VScript. We will start from the absolute beginning—assuming you have no prior programming experience—and build your skills step-by-step. Our goal is to empower you to turn your creative puzzle ideas into interactive reality.

For experienced developers, the initial chapters on programming fundamentals will be a quick refresher. Feel free to use the table of contents to jump to the sections on Squirrel syntax and the Portal 2 API.

How This Book is Structured:

  • Part 0: The Language of Logic (Programming Fundamentals): We'll learn the universal concepts of coding in a way that anyone can understand.
  • Part 1: Learning the Dialect (Introduction to Squirrel): We'll focus on the specific grammar and rules of Squirrel, the language used in Portal 2's VScript.
  • Part 2: Connecting to the World (VScript in Portal 2): We'll bridge the gap between your code and the game itself, learning how to make scripts run.
  • Part 3: Becoming a Creator (Interacting with the Game): The core of Portal 2 modding. You'll learn to find, create, and manipulate every object in the game.
  • Part 4: Advanced Techniques (PCapture-Lib & Beyond): Once you've mastered the basics, we'll introduce a powerful community-made library to supercharge your scripts.

Let's start our journey.
